Privacy Policy for ARgility Go Last Updated: July 20, 2026 We, Trainality, operates the ARgility Go mobile application. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, including athletes, clinicians, and physical therapists. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of data within the Application. By downloading and using ARgility Go, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. 1. Information We Collect and How It Is Used A. Camera Access and Image Processing ARgility Go requires access to your device’s camera to function. The camera feed is used exclusively in real time to provide motion tracking, agility metrics, and reaction-time feedback during performance drills. No Storage of Images or Video: The Application processes camera frames instantly in volatile memory to detect movement. No images, video recordings, or visual data are ever recorded, saved to the device’s permanent storage, or transmitted over the internet. Local Processing: All computer vision and image processing happen entirely on your local device. B. Athlete Profile Data and Performance Metrics (Cloud Stored) To track athletic progression, manage rehabilitation records, and sync history across sessions, the Application collects and stores the following data points: Name or Athlete Identifier Age, Sex, and Primary Sport / Medical Diagnosis Drill Performance Metrics (e.g., specific reaction times, timestamps, completion speeds, and personal bests) 2. Data Transmission and Secure Cloud Storage Unlike the camera video processing, athlete profiles and historical drill times are synced to a secure cloud network to ensure data persistence. Secure Cloud Database: Athlete drill times and profile metrics are securely transmitted and stored on an encrypted cloud database infrastructure. This allows you to safely maintain accurate records across multiple training sessions or devices. Data Protection: We utilize industry-standard encryption protocols (such as HTTPS and SSL/TLS) to secure all data in transit between the Application and our cloud databases. No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, trade, or distribute your performance data or personally identifiable information (PII) to third-party advertisers, data brokers, or external companies. Cloud storage is utilized strictly for your own internal app functionality and metric tracking. 3. Data Retention and User Control We store your training history and cloud metrics for as long as your account or profile remains active to provide continuous progress tracking. If you wish to have your account, profile, or accumulated drill times permanently deleted from our cloud database, you can request data deletion by contacting us at the developer support email listed below. 4. Children’s Privacy ARgility Go is designed to track athletic performance. If the Application is used by or for individuals under the age of 13, any data entered (such as name or age) remains strictly on the local device under the control of the primary user or clinician, and is subject to the same strict local-only protections outlined above. 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. 6.
